Welcome to the World of Nature

Nature is the most beautiful and attractive surrounding around us which make us happy and provide us a natural environment to live healthy. It includes everything like trees, plants, animals, rivers, lakes, birds, sky, mountains, forests and more.

Why Nature is Important?

Nature provides us with everything we need: food, water, shelter, air, and much more. Preserving nature is not only our duty but a necessity for our survival.
