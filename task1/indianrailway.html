Welcome to Indian Railway

Indian Railways is one of the largest and busiest rail networks in the world. It plays a crucial role in connecting different parts of the country and supports the transportation of millions of passengers and tonnes of goods every day.

History and Importance

Established in 1853, the Indian Railways has grown tremendously and now operates under the Ministry of Railways, Government of India. It is a symbol of unity, connecting cities, towns, and villages across the country. The railway is not only a means of transport but also a lifeline for trade, tourism, and employment.
